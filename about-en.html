ABOUT

Orchestra Lartife

Founded in 2024, Orchestra Lartife is a professional ensemble bringing together musicians active in leading municipal and professional orchestras in Korea and abroad.

Grounded in the essence and depth of classical music, the orchestra seeks a close and genuine connection with audiences. Its programs range from the core repertoire to thoughtfully conceived contemporary productions, creating a concert culture in which classical music can be understood and enjoyed naturally.

Through collaborations with distinguished arts organizations—including the Incheon City Chorale and Suwon City Chorale—the orchestra has earned recognition for its musical quality. From chamber music to large orchestral works, and through partnerships with choirs, ballet companies, and other artistic disciplines, Orchestra Lartife continually explores new musical possibilities.

Under the artistic leadership of conductor Panju Kim, Orchestra Lartife creates stages that connect people and art beyond the act of performance itself. Its mission is to bring music naturally into everyday life through performances where tradition and innovation coexist.
