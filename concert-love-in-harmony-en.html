Love in Harmony

Multicultural Community Autumn Concert

Date
9 November 2024 · 3:00 & 7:00 PM
Venue
Underwood Memorial Hall
Conductor
Panju Kim
Orchestra

About

An autumn gala created with the multicultural community, bringing orchestra, vocal music, piano, musical theater, choir, and ballet together on one stage. Orchestra Lartife connected diverse artistic genres to complete a colorful program that audiences of all backgrounds could enjoy together.
