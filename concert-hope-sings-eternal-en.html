Hope Sings Eternal

Incheon City Chorale · 191st Regular Concert

Date
11 June 2025 · 7:30 PM
Venue
Arts Center Incheon · Concert Hall
Guest Conductor
Dr. Pearl Shangkuan
Orchestra
Orchestra Lartife

About

The Incheon City Chorale’s 191st regular concert was shaped around the theme of hope re-emerging beyond suffering and despair. Orchestra Lartife joined the chorale in works including Samuel Barber’s Agnus Dei and Dan Forrest’s Requiem for the Living, creating a profound and expansive choral-orchestral sound.
